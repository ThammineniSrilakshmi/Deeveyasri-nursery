About Us

Deeveyasri Nursery is a family-run plant nursery based in Eluru, offering a wide variety of plants to beautify your surroundings and purify your air. We specialize in fruit plants, indoor greens, flowering plants, and medicinal herbs. With love and care, we nurture every plant so it finds a happy home with you!
